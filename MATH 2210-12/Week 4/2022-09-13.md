	### $$f(x)=ax^N$$
### $$f'(x)=aNx^{N-1}$$
### Examples
###### Problem 1
find $y'$ for $y=x^{9.2}$
$y=x^{9.2}$
$\boxed{y=9.2x^{8.2}}$

###### Problem 2
find $f'(x)$ for $f(x)=\frac{1}{x^{16}}$
$f(x)=x^{-16}$
$\boxed{f(x)=-16x^{-17}}$

###### Problem 4
find $f'(x)$ for $f(x)=x^\pi$
$f(x)=x^\pi$
$\boxed{f(x)=\pi x^{\pi-1}}$

###### Problem 5
find $f'(t)$ for $f(t)=7t^2-7t+15$
$f(t)=7t^2-7t+15$
$\boxed{f(t)=14t-7+0}$

###### Problem 6
find $y'$ for $y=6t^{15}-12\sqrt{t}+\frac{4}{t}$
$y=6t^{15}-12\sqrt{t}+\frac{4}{t}$
$y=6t^{15}-12t^3+4t^{-1}$
$\boxed{y=90t^{14}-36t^2-4t^{-2}}$

###### Problem 7
find $y'$ for $\sqrt{x}(x^3+7)$
