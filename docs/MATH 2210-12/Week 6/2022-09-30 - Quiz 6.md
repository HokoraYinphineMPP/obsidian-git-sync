if $w(r)=\dfrac{3r+5}{\sqrt{r^2+12}}$
find $w'(r)$

###### Quotient Rule
$\dfrac{w'(r)*g(r)-g'(r)*w(r)}{(w(r))^2}$



$g(r)=\sqrt{r^2+12}$
$g(r)=(r^2+12)^{\frac{1}{2}}$
$g'(r)=\frac{1}{2}(r^2+12)^{-\frac{1}{2}}$

$w(r)=3r+5$
$w'(r)=3$


$\dfrac{\frac{d}{dr}(3r+5)*\sqrt{r^2+12}-(3r+5)*\frac{d}{dr}\left(\sqrt{r^2+12}\right)}{\left(\sqrt{r^2+12}\right)^2}$
