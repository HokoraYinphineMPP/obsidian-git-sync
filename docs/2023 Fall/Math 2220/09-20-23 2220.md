$\int_{0}^{2}xe^{x^2}dx$
> $u=x^2$
> $du=2xdx$
> $\frac{1}{2}du=xdx$

$=\int_{0}^{2}e^{x^2}xdx$
$=\int_{x=0}^{x=2}e^u\left(\frac{1}{2}du\right)$
$=\frac{1}{2}\int_{x=0}^{x=2}e^udu$
$=\frac{1}{2}e^u\biggr|_{x=0}^{x=2}$
$=\frac{1}{2}e^{x^2}\biggr|_{0}^{2}$
